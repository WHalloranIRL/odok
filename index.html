About Us!

Welcome to O'Donnacha's of Kilmeaden, where the heart of Ireland's countryside meets the warmth of community and the richness of culinary tradition. Our gastropub is a haven for those seeking a taste of authenticity.


Our menu caters for everyone and uses the finest locally sourced ingredients. With a commitment to hospitality as hearty as our dishes, we invite you to savor the charm of our rustic ambiance and experience the true spirit of Irish hospitality at O'Donnacha's.
